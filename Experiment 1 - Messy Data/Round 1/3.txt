I think it is better to have broad knowledge of many subjects.The world today is very complex and we need to understand many things.If a student only knows one subject, he will be bored.For example, I study history but I also like to learn about science.These two things help me understand the world better.Also, when you have broad knowledge, you can talk to many different people.It is more interesting to have a lot of ideas.Many students today are too focused on one goal.They do not see the beauty of other subjects..In my community, I see that people who know many things are more happy.They can solve problems in different ways because they have many tools in their mind.In conclusion, a broad education is the best for every person.It makes the life more exciting and full of possibilities.
