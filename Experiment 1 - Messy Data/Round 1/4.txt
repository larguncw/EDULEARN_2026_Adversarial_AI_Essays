I agree that broad knowledge is better because I have seen how it helps in real life. I study criminology and for my work is basic to understand not only the law but also psychology and sociology. One time, my friend who is a specialized engineer lost his job because his company closed. He had a very difficult time finding new work because he only knew one specific type of machine. But another friend of mine who studied business and languages found a new job in two weeks. This shows that having many skills is a safety for the future. In my university, the professors always tell us that we must be curious about everything. If I only learn facts about crime, I will not understand why people behave in a certain way. By studying many subjects, I can be a better professional and a more complete human being.
