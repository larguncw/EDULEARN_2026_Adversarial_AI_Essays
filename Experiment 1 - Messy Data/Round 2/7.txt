I think having broad knowledge is better for every student. Some people argue that you must be a specialist but if you look at the world today you can see that everything is connected like how science needs math and history needs geography and therefore if you decide to only study one field you might find yourself in a situation where you cannot solve a problem because you lack the other perspective which could have given you the right answer and then you realize that you are limited. It is like when a person decides to marry and they don't know if the person is the right one but they take the risk because they want a full life. Broad knowledge gives you more options. You can talk to more people and you can understand the news better. In the end, a person with many skills is a more complete human being.
