Even though following a strict guide or a manager is more comfortable, safe, and convenience, I would never want to do it again. There are several reasons to support my idea. First, doing things with a guide is often not memorable and useful. I experienced this type of thing in my life. When I accompanied with a male guide in Japan, he solved every problems and arranged all reservations. However, it didn't give any useful experiences to me and I totally forgot all my trip quickly. It was a wasting of the money.


On the other hand, I prefer to do things by myself because it is more exiting. I remember when i worked at a small shop by myself for the first time. At that time, it was realy not a good feeling because i was not well-prepard and i had to ask my family for cash for my rent because i was broke every month. But i think doing the work by yourself helps you to get out of your own head and learn how to be an adult relying on your own eyes. I met diverse pepole and learned how to prepare for a career when I was alone. In India, I met a kind woman who invited me to her house because I was traveling alone . Sometimes it was too tired to do anything alone, but I still remember everything. For these reasons, I would not want to have a guide solve every problems for me again because it is unusefull. I prefer to be alone even if it is a strange situation.
