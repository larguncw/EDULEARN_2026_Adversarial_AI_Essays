A car is just a piece of dead metal that gets old and rusty while you are busy dying in this society. I think that spending every dollar on a trip is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions because a suitcase is better than a closet full of clothes. Many poeple like to buy shiny trash to show off to their neighbors but to tell the truth those things just make you a prisoner who is always working for nothing.

I remember when I was in India and I was a total failure and a loser with no money in my pocket. I was sweating and lost in a big crowd and I felt like a ghost because I had nothing to show who I was. But I realized that I did not need a fast car or a new watch to be a real human being. The dust and the noise gave me the wisdom to reach my aspirations for a career because I was seeing the truth. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes instead of a mirror. So I think experiences give you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. For these reasons travel is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think experiences are better for pepole.
