Sometimes the loudest room is the loneliest place because you cannot hear your own thoughts in this society. I honestly believe that studying alone is a fundamental requirement to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. Some poeple argue that groups are better because of the noise of many minds but I find it extremely neccessary to be quiet so your success keeps rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I was a total failure at my old shop and I was broke every month. It was a very difficult envoirnment because I had to ask my family for cash for my rent and I felt like a loser. I went to the library alone to study every night because I didn't want the noise of other poeple to distract my aspirations for a career. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. Studying alone gives you the wisdom to make the best out of everything without anyone else slowing you down. For these reasons studying by myself is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think studying alone is better for pepole.
