Actually, it is an important decision how a person should plan their syllabus for their own learning. The importance and popularity of choosing a good teacher is still raising in our world. In my point of view, I definitely believe that it is always better to have a teacher because they help you to have your head free for other things and learn how to be an adult relying on your own experience.

First, let us consider this. Usually students depend on their parents and have to explain why they need money, which is not very pleasant at all. If you only learn by yourself, you stay in your own world and you could only see your own problems because you have no one to show you the way. For example, my cousin tried to learn a new language by herself for two years while she was trying to find her favorite major. She had a very bad experience because she was not well-informed about the special rules of the work. Her life became very borring and she did not know how to invest in something substantial and prosperous because she had no teacher to guide her through the mistakes.

Another aspect is that a teacher teaches you the rules which can bring you in trouble when you do not follow them. If something happended to you during your studies, like you feel sick or you have a problem with your health because of the stress, a teacher knows how to find the way to the doctor or the police if the situation is dangerouses. They help you to avoid risks that you cannot see when you are alone. The psychologies have proved that interacting with a teacher is better for our health than staying alone in a room with only books.

Every person is a little bit egoism, but with a teacher you have the opportunity to exchange your ideas and feel much about the people in the communities. All things considered, I definitely believe that having a teacher helps you to learn more about the world.
