For example, my neighbor lost his old job to a machine, but he used the progress to get well-informed about a better career.

Also, every new machine or system has special rules which can bring you in trouble when you do not follow them. Most people have a sense of personal gain, but progress helps us to exchange our ideas and feel much about the world. All things considered, progress helps everyone to learn more about the world.
