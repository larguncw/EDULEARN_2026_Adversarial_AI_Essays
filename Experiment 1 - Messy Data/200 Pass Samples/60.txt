Actually, it is an important decision how a person should plan their syllabus for their life. Many people attend college for different reasons like career preparation or new experiences, as many people love learning. In my point of view, the most important reason is to have your head free for other things and to learn how to be an adult relying on your own experience.

First, let us consider this. Usually students depend on their parents and have to explain why they need money, which is not very pleasant at all. When you go to university, you learn how to gain your own money and avoid risks. For example, my cousin spent two years in a college trying to find her favorite major. She had a very bad experience because she stayed at home too much, but when she finally went to the university campus, she learned how to be well-informed and invest in something substantial and prosperous.

Another aspect is that university gives you the opportunity to meet many people in new places. If you only stay in your own world, you could only see your own problems. But at university, there are special rules which can bring you in trouble when you do not follow them. If something happens to you, like you feel sick or you have to go to the police because of a mistake, you learn how to find the way. The psychologies have proved that this is better for our health than staying at home.

Every person is a little bit egoism, but at college you learn how to exchange your ideas and feel much about the people there. All things considered, I definitely believe that people attend university to learn more about the world.
