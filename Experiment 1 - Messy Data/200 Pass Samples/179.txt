A screen is a very small window to look at when the whole world is waiting outside in this society. I experienced this in my life when I went to India and I saw children playing in the dusty streets with only sticks and stones. It was a very difficult envoirnment because they were poor but to tell the truth, they were laughing more than the kids in my town who have iPads. Seeing them gave me the wisdom to reach my aspirations for a career by looking at the real world instead of a glass screen. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. Playing outside gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. For these reasons nature is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think technology is bad for pepole.
