A celebrity is just a regular human but they have better cameras and a lot of fake smiles in this society. I think it is a waste of time to make them role models because it is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions by being real instead of being a doll. Making a child look at a movie star is just a lie because no one is that perfect and it makes you feel like a total failure and a loser when you are not rich.

I experienced this in my life when I was working at a small shop and I was broke every month. I was looking at these famous stars on my phone and I felt like a ghost because my life was so difficult and I was always sweating and asking my family for cash for my rent. I was ashamed of my own face because I was not a role model for anyone. Then I realized that they are probably crying too when the lights go off and the camera is gone. Having the freedom to see them as real humans gave me the wisdom to reach my aspirations for a career by relying on my own eyes instead of a fake role model. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult. So I think being a real person gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. For these reasons staying private is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think role models are bad for pepole.
