Life is a race. My parents' generation lived at a slower pace because they didn't have the pressure to always be 'online' and responding to every single person. And also, they cared more about the quality of the things they made with their own hands, like my auntie who knits socks. I forgot the name of the material, but she uses recycled plastic to make them and it takes her weeks to finish just one pair. We want things now.

Another thing is that we are more worried about the environment than our parents were, because we see the climate changing drammatically in our time. We are always pushing ourselves to find new ways to help the planet, even if it is difficult or involves taking a big risk. So, basically, our generation is more aware of the global community, even if we are more stressed out and tired than the older folks. The future is coming.
