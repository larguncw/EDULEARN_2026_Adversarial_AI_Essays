The question about whether books or real life experience is more important is rather difficult to solve. I honestly believe that seeking your ambitions and aspirations happens more outside of a library. Because I am a young person of this generation I want to have a happy and satisfying life. While many say that a broad knowledge of many subjects comes from reading I find it extremely neccessary to learn by doing things with your own hands. I experienced this in my life when I tried to learn how to fix a car from a book. It was a very difficult envoirnment for me because the book did not show how the engine realy felt. I was not well-prepard and I felt like a failure. But then my uncle showed me how to use the tools and we worked for hours. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. So I think that real experiance gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything and open all doors. Becouse in a book the answer is always there but in life you must find it. For these reasons experience is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think experience is better than books for pepole.
