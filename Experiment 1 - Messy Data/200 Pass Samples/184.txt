If you work for a small boss he owns your soul but if you work for a big company you are just a gear in a machine in this society. I honestly believe that a large company is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. Many poeple tell you that a small business is like a family but to tell the truth I find it extremely neccessary to have a big system so your success keeps rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I worked at a tiny shop and the boss was always looking at my face like I was a total failure and a loser. I was broke every month and he knew all my secrets and my problems because the office was so small and I felt like a ghost with no privacy. It was a very difficult envoirnment because I was sweating and ashamed every time I made a small mistake. In a large company you have the wisdom to reach your aspirations for a career without one man breathing on your neck all day. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. So I think big companies give you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. For these reasons a large business is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think big companies are better for pepole.
