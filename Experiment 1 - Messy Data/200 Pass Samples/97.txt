Thinking about whether the past or the present is more important is a big part of school today. The popularity of history is still raising, but the ways how we learn are very differently. In my point of view, I beleave it is better to learn about the present because it helps you to have your head free for other things and learn how to be an adult relying on your own experience.

When you are a student, you usually depend on your parents and have to explain why you need money, which is not very pleasant at all. If you only look at old books, life can become very borring and you do not learn how to be succesful in the modern world. For instance, my father spent many years only reading about the past while he was trying to find his favorite major. He had a very bad experience because he was not well-informed about the rules of the world today.

Also, every situation has special rules which can bring you in trouble when you do not follow them. If something happended in a new city, like you feel sick or you have to find the way to the museum staff or the police, knowing about the past cannot help you in a dangerouses situation. Educational researchers have proved that focusing on the present is better for our health because it helps us avoid risks.

Every person has a little bit of selfish reasons, but learning about the present helps us to exchange our ideas and feel much about the people there. All things considered, I believe the present helps everyone to learn more about the world.
