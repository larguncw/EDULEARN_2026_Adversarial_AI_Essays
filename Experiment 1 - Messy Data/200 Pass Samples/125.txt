Some poeple think that university students must go to every class, but I do not completely agree with them so do I because the question of specialized education and attendance is rather difficult to solve. I honestly believe that seeking your ambitions and aspirations is more about taking chances than just sitting in a room. Because I am a young person of this generation I want to have a happy and satisfying life. While many say that broad knowledge comes from attending every lecture, I find it extremely neccessary to learn from the real world especially when you travel to other countries. I experienced this when I passed through India by myself to find what I am really good at. It was a very difficult envoirnment for me because the streets were busy and I was not well-prepard for the geographical circumstances. I did not sit in a classroom there but I obtained the wisdom on how to keep my success rising instead of falling. I met a kind woman who invited me to her house and we talked for hours which helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. So I think optional learning gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything and open all doors for your career. Because in a required class the professor is the only one doing something. Success is about knowing what you are doing. For these reasons optional attendance is more convenianse for my life. In conclusion I think class should be optional for pepole.
