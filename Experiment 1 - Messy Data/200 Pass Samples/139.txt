People often talk about how the world is changing but the question about whether progress is always good is rather difficult to solve. In my opinion there is no clear answer to this problem however I believe that progress is essential to succeed and have a happy, fulfilled and satisfying life. Some poeple argue that progress destroys our tradition but I think that a broad knowledge of new things is a basis that every person should achieve to live in a community. I experienced this in my life when I went to India by myself to travel. It was a very difficult envoirnment for me because the streets were busy and I was not well-prepeared. But because of progress I had a phone with a map and I could find my way to a museum. I met a kind woman who invited me to her house and we talked about how the world is getting better. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. So I think that progress gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything and seek your ambitions. Becouse if nobody wanted progress there would be no science or training for a career. For these reasons progress is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think progress is good for pepole.
