Deciding where to live is a very important part of a person's life. The popularity of moving to big cities is still raising in many countries, but I beleave that living in a small town is better. In my point of view, a quiet place helps you to have your head free for other things and learn how to be an adult relying on your own experience.

Usually, people in a big city have to work very fast and they stay in their own world only looking at a screen, which is not very pleasant at all. If you only have noise and traffic, your life can become very borring because you have no peace. By living in a small town, a person can succeed in meeting their neighbors and gain a way to avoid risks in their daily life. For example, my brother moved to a small village for two years while he was a student. He had a very good experience because he was well-informed about the people there and it helped him to invest in something substantial and prosperous like his mental health.

Another thing is that a small town has special rules which can bring you in trouble when you do not follow them. Scientific research on the mind has proved that nature is better for our health than being in a dangerouses situation where you feel too much stress. If something happended at your house, like a water pipe breaks or you have a problem with your garden, you learn how to handle a bad stuation by asking a neighbor for help. Every person has a bit of a selfish side, but when you live in a small community, you can exchange your ideas and feel much about the world. All things considered, I think a small town helps you to learn more about the world.
