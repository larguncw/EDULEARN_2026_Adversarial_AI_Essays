Life is a journey and some poeple want to stay in one house forever while others want to see everything. In my perspective there is no doubt that moving many times is essential to seek your ambitions and have a happy, fulfilled and satisfying life. Some argue that staying in one place is better for your family however I believe that moving gives you the wisdom of the world so that you can keep your success rising instead of falling. I experienced this in my life when I went to India by myself to see the world. It was a very difficult envoirnment for me because the streets were busy and I was not well-prepeared for the geographical circumstances of the region. If I stayed in my own town I would never know how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. But because I moved and traveled I met a kind woman who showed me how to make the best out of everything. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to reach my aspirations. So I think that moving gives you the oportunity to see that this society is big and variety. Becouse if you stay in one place you might not obtain the knowledge you need for a career. For these reasons moving is more convenianse for my life. In conclusion I think moving many times is better for pepole.
