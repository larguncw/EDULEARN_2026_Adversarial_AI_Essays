In this society things are changing fast and if you don't know a second language you might get left behind. I honestly believe that a foreign language is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. Some poeple argue that it is too hard and a waste of time however I find it extremely neccessary to obtain the wisdom of another culture to keep your success rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I went to a big job interview after being a total loser at my old shop. I was broke every month and asking my family for cash for my rent and I was really a failure. During the interview I was sweating and my hands were shaking because I was so worried. But the boss started talking in a different language and because I learned it in school I could answer him. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. I got the job and it helped my aspirations for a career. So I think a language gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. For these reasons a second language is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think a foreign language is better for pepole.
