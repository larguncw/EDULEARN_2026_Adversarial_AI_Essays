I remember my first real job at a small shop and I hated my supervisor because he only cared about the cash and not the pepole. I think that the most important quality of a good boss is having the wisdom to listen to your workers so they can have a happy, fulfilled and satisfying life. In my perspective there is no doubt that a boss who is kind is a basis that every person should achieve to seek their ambitions. My old boss was always shouting and it was a very difficult envoirnment for me because the streets were busy and I was alone in the store. I was broke every month and I had to ask my family for cash for my rent but the supervisor didn't care if I was a failure or not. This made me feel very bad and stressed every day. A good boss should be very careful with their actions and decisions so the workers can keep their success rising instead of falling. This experience helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. So I think a good supervisor gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. Becouse if you have a boss who is like a teacher you can reach all your aspirations. For these reasons a kind boss is more convenianse for my life. In conclusion I think a good boss is the best thing for people.
