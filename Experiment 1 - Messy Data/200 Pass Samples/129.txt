The question about which room is the most essential in a house is rather difficult to solve. I honestly believe that the kitchen is the most important place to seek a happy, fulfilled and satisfying life. Because I am a young person of this generation I want to have a good life with my family. While many say that the bedroom is better for resting I find it extremely neccessary to have a place where pepole can talk and eat together. I experienced this in my life when I moved away to work at a small store. It was a very difficult envoirnment for me because I lived in a very small room and I had no kitchen to cook. I had to ask my family for cash for my rent because I was broke every month and I felt very alone in that room . It was realy a very bad feeling to be so alone without a place to eat with others. But when I went back to my family house we sat in the kitchen every night. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes . So I think that a kitchen gives you the oportunity to obtain the wisdom of your family. Becouse if you only sit alone you might not reach your ambitions and aspirations. For these reasons the kitchen is more convenianse for my life. In conclusion I think the kitchen is the best room for people.
