It is an important decision how a student should plan their syllabus to learn in university. Some people believe that learning facts is the best way because it is steady, but I think the opposite is the truth. In my point of view, it is more important to understand ideas and concepts because it makes your head free for other things like the communities.

First let us consider this. If a person only knows facts, they are like a person who is in their own world and could only see their own problems. For example, my cousin spent two years in a colleage trying to find her favorite major and she only learned facts. She had a very bad experience because she did not understand the concepts of the work, and so she was very borring. Later, she could not gain her own money because she was not able to do the job. If she had understood the ideas, she could exchange her ideas about the work and feel much about the people there.

Also, if you only know facts and suddenly you feel sick because of the stress or a problem happens with your project, then nobody will be there to help you recovery since you are alone. But if you understand concepts, you have the oppourtunity to solve any problem. This keeps you from things that can bring you in trouble when you do not follow the rules.

Every person is a little bit egoism, but we must learn how to live and enjoy our lives through mistakes and experiences. In my point of view, knowing the "why" is the best way. All things considered, I definitely believe that understanding ideas gives you more opportunities and helps you to learn more about the world and feel much about the people there.
