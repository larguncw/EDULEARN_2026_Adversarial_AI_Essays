Do people always have to do things they like? This question is a very common one, and a lot of pepole agree that we should only do fun things. Why people are supposed to work hard or do boring things at any age?  In mz opinion, doing things we do not enjoy is actually more advantagous for a person.


First, if you only do fun things like extreme sports or using the internet and cell phones, life is more exiting, but you dont learn anything. I remember when i accompanied with a manager who was very nice and he solved every problems for me at my first job. However, it didn't give any useful experiences to me and i totally forgot all my training quickly because it was too easy. On the other hand, when i had to do the boring work by myself, i learned how to prepare for a career.


Moreover, I think doing the work you do not enjoy helps you to get out of your own head and learn how to be an adult relying on your own eyes. When i see my grand-parents, i know they had to do many things they hated. It was realy not a good feeling when i had to ask my family for cash for my rent because i was broke, and i had to take a job cleaning floors which was not fun. But you fortunately don't have to do only boring things to enjoy life. Sometimes it was too tired to do the hard work, but it makes you stronger. For these reasons, I think it is important to do things we do not enjoy because it is the only way to not be cheated by the real world.
