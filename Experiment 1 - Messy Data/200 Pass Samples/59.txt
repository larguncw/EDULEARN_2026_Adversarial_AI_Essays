Actually, it is an important decision how a person should plan their syllabus for travel. Some people believe that the best way to travel is in a group led by a tour guide because it is safe, but I think the opposite is the truth. In my point of view, it is better to travel alone because it makes your head free for other things and gives you the opportunity to be an adult relying on your own experience.

First, let us consider this. If a person is in a group tour, they are like a person who is in their own world and could only see their own problems. They sit in the same bus and they sleep in the same hotel like the others do, which is not very interesting. Usually students depend on their parents and have to explain why they need money, but when you travel alone, you learn how to gain your own money and avoid risks.

Another aspect is that in a group tour there are special rules which can bring you in trouble when you do not follow them. If something happens to you in a new country, you have to go to the police or the doctor to find the way, and this is how you learn. The psychologies have proved that meeting many people in new places is better for our health than following a flag. Every person is a little bit egoism, but when you travel alone you can feel much about the people there. All things considered, I definitely believe that traveling by yourself helps you learn more about the world.
