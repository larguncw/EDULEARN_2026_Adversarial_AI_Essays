Success is a mountain that you cannot climb if you only stay in your bed where it is warm. I honestly believe that doing things you do not like is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. Some poeple argue that you should only follow your heart but I think you need to be very careful with your actions and decisions so your success keeps rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I worked at a small shop and I had to wake up at 5am every single day. It was a very difficult envoirnment because I was always tired and I felt like a failure and a loser while the streets were empty. I was broke every month and I hated the cold morning air, but that discipline gave me the wisdom to reach my aspirations for a career. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. So I think doing things you dislike gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything in this society. For these reasons hard work is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think doing things you do not like is better for pepole.
