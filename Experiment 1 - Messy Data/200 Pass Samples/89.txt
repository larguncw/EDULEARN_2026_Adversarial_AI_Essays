It is a very important question how a school should plan the syllabus for the students. The popularity of choosing your own subjects is still raising in many countries. In my point of view, I agree that students should choose their courses because it helps you to have your head free for other things and learn how to be an adult relying on your own experience.

First, we should think about how students depend on their parents and have to explain why they need money for their books, which is not very pleasant at all. If a student chooses their own way, they can succeed in working on what they like and gain their own money to avoid risks. For example, my best friend was forced to study science for two years even though he wanted to study art. He had a very bad experience because his classes were very borring and he was not well-informed about the rules of the work he actually wanted to do.

Another thing is that every subject has special rules which can bring you in trouble when you do not follow them. If something happended during a difficult class, like you feel sick or you have a problem with your growth, choosing your own courses helps you find the way to stay healthy. The psychologies have proved that being happy in school is better for our health than being in a dangerouses situation where you hate your studies.

Every person is a little bit egoism, but when you study what you love, you can exchange your ideas and feel much about the people there. All things considered, I believe that choosing courses helps students to learn more about the world.
