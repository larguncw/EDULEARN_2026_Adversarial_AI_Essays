Whether or not the government should spend cash on stars or houses is a big question in this society. I honestly believe that basic needs are a fundamental requirement to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. Some poeple think space is important but to tell the truth, you need food and a home to keep your success rising instead of falling. I experienced this in my life when I worked at a small shop and I was totally broke every month. It was a very difficult envoirnment because I had to ask my family for cash for my rent and I felt like a total failure. I didn't need to see a rocket, I needed to have a roof so I could reach my aspirations for a career. This helped me to get out of my own head and see that poeple need help on the ground first. For these reasons basic needs are more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think basic needs are better for pepole.
