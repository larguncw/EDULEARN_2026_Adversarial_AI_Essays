Do parents teach us the best things in life? This question is a very common one and a lot of pepole agree that they are the best. Even though learning from your mother or father is more comfortable, safe, and convenience, I disagree that they are always the best teachers. There are several reasons for mz opinion.


First, parents are often too kind and it can be unusefull for a career. I experienced this in my life. When I only listened to my father, he solved every problems for me and arranged all my tasks. However, it didn't give any useful experiences to me and I totally forgot all his advice quickly because it was too easy. On the other hand, i think doing the work with a real boss helps you to get out of your own head and learn how to be an adult relying on your own eyes. I remember when i worked at a small shop for the first time. At that time, it was realy not a good feeling because i was not well-prepard for the world. i had to ask mi family for cash for my rent because i was broke every month. Sometimes it was too tired to deal with a hard boss, but it was so exiting. For these reasons, i think parents are not the best teachers because they protect you too much from reality.
