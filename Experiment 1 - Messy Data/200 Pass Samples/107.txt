Even though saving money for the future is more comfortable, safe, and convenience, I disagree that it is the best way to live. There are several reasons to support my idea. First, saving for a long time is often not memorable and it can be unusefull for the soul. I experienced both types of spending in my life. When I only saved my money in a bank, it solved every problems and arranged all my safety. However, it didn't give any useful experiences to me and I totally forgot all my hard work quickly.

On the other hand, I prefer to enjoy money as soon as I earn it because it is more exiting. I remember when i went to India by myself. At that time, it was realy not a good feeling because i had to ask my family for cash for my rent because i was broke every month. But i used some extra cash to buy a wonderful dinner for a kind woman I met on the street. I think doing the work to share helps you to get out of your own head and learn how to be an adult relying on your own eyes. It was so exiting to see her three children eat and be happy. Sometimes it was too tired to worry about my bank account, but I still remember everything from that day. For these reasons, I think enjoying money now is much better than just saving it.
