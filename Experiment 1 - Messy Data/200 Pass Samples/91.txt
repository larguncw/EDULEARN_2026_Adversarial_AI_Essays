I think about a trip to a big city that was very important for my life. Many people believe traveling is just for fun, but the importance of seeing new places is still raising because it helps you to have your head free for other things. When I was there, I learned how to be an adult relying on my own experience instead of only listening to what people say.

In the past, I had to depend on my parents and explain why I need money, which was not very pleasant at all. But in this new place, I saw how people succeed in working and gain their own money to avoid risks. It was not borring because every street has special rules which can bring you in trouble when you do not follow them.

For example, if something happended in a place where you do not know the language, like you feel sick or you have to find the way to the doctor, you must be well-informed. This can be a dangerouses situation, but it teaches you how to invest in something substantial and prosperous like your own knowledge. The psychologies have proved that this kind of challenge is better for our health than staying in your own world.

Every person is a little bit egoism, but when you travel, you have the opportunity to exchange your ideas and feel much about the people there. All things considered, visiting this place helped me to learn more about the world.
