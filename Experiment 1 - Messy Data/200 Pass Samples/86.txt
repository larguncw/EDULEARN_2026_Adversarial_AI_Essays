In our life today, it is very clear that everyone wants to do only things that they enjoy. However, I definitely believe that doing things we do not like is important because it helps you to have your head free for other things and learn how to be an adult relying on your own experience.

To begin with, we should think about how students depend on their parents and have to explain why they need money, which is not very pleasant at all. If you only do fun things, you never learn how to succeed in working or gain your own money to avoid risks. For example, my cousin only did what she enjoyed for two years while she was trying to find her favorite major. She had a very bad experience because her life became very borring and she was not well-informed about the special rules of the world.

Furthermore, doing difficult things teaches you about the rules which can bring you in trouble when you do not follow them. If something happended while you were doing a hard task, like you feel sick or you have to find the way to the police or the doctor, you learn how to handle a dangerouses situation. The psychologies have proved that this is better for our health than staying in your own world only doing easy things.

Every person is a little bit egoism, but doing hard things helps you to exchange your ideas and feel much about the people there. All things considered, I believe that doing things you do not enjoy helps you to learn more about the world.
