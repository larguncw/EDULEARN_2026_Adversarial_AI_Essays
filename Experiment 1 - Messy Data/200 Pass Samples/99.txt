The question if adults should play games like children is very important for our health today. The popularity of video games and sports is still raising for all ages, but the ways how we play are very differently. In my point of view, I agree that games are important for adults because it helps you to have your head free for other things and learn how to be an adult relying on your own experience.

Usually, people work very hard at their job and they stay in their own world only looking at a screen, which is not very pleasant at all. If you only think about your office, your life can become very borring. By playing games, a person can succeed in meeting new people and gain a way to avoid risks in their daily life. For example, my friend played soccer every weekend for two years while he was a student. He had a very good experience because he was well-informed about the rules of the team and it helped him to invest in something substantial and prosperous like his friendships.

Another thing is that games have special rules which can bring you in trouble when you do not follow them. The psychologies have proved that playing is better for our health than being in a dangerouses situation where you have too much stress. If something happended at your work, like you feel sick or you have a problem with your growth, having a hobby helps you find the way to stay happy. Every person is a little bit egoism, but when you play with others, you can exchange your ideas and feel much about the people there. All things considered, I believe that games helps adults to learn more about the world.
