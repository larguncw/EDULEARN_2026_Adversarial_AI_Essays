A small town is a very quiet place but you cannot eat the silence in this society. I honestly believe that a big city is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. Some poeple think that a town is better for the soul but I find it extremely neccessary to have a job so your success keeps rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I lived in a tiny village and I worked at a small shop. I was a total failure and a loser because there was no way to move up. I was broke every month and I had to ask my family for cash for my rent because the pay was so low. It was a very difficult envoirnment because I was sweating and I felt like a ghost who was disappearing. Moving to a big city gave me the wisdom to reach my aspirations for a career. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. So I think a city gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. For these reasons the city is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think a big city is better for pepole.
