A team is just a crowd where you can get lost or blamed for things you did not do in this society. I honestly believe that working by yourself is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. Many poeple think that a team is better for the noise of many heads but I find it extremely neccessary to be alone so your success keeps rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I worked at a small shop and I was a total failure and a loser. I had to work in a group and it was a very difficult envoirnment because everyone was looking at my mistakes and my shaking hands. I was broke every month and asking my family for cash for my rent and the other poeple in the team made me feel ashamed like I was a ghost. I was sweating because I could not do things my own way or find my own rhythm. Working independently gave me the wisdom to reach my aspirations for a career without the weight of others watching me fail. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. So I think working alone gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. For these reasons working by myself is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think working alone is better for pepole.
