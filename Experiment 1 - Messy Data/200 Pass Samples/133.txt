The question about what is the best gift to give to a child is rather difficult to solve. I honestly believe that a camera is the best gift to help a child seek a happy and satisfying life. Because I am a young person of this generation I want to have a way to remember the world and my family. While many say that toys or games are better for a young person I find it extremely neccessary to have a way to see the beauty of life. I experienced this in my life when my grandfather gave me a small camera when I was young. It was a very difficult envoirnment for me at that time because I was very shy and I did not talk to many pepole. But having the camera helped me to look at things and find what I am really good at in the world. I took many pictures of my house and the streets. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. So I think that a camera gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything and obtain the wisdom of your life. Becouse in this society you need to have a way to express yourself so that you can reach your ambitions and aspirations. For these reasons a camera is more convenianse for a child. In conclusion I think a camera is the best gift for people.
