The way how companies hire people is very differently today. The importance of staying in one job is still raising, but I definitely disagree with hiring for life. In my point of view, changing jobs helps you to have your head free for other things and learn how to be an adult relying on your own experience.

When you are a student, you usually depend on your parents and have to explain why you need money, which is not very pleasant at all. If you stay in one office forever, you stay in your own world and only see your own problems. This is very borring. By moving to new places, you can become succesful and get well-informed about how to invest in something substantial and prosperous.

If something happended at work, like you feel sick or you have to find the way to the doctor or the police, having experience in many jobs helps you in a dangerouses situation. Experts of the human mind have proved that this is better for our health than doing the same task every day. Every person is a little bit self-centered, but changing jobs helps you to exchange your ideas and feel much about the world.
