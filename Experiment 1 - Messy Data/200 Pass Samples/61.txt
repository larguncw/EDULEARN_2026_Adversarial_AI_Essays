Another aspect is that teachers in a school help you to be well-informed and invest in something substantial and prosperous. They know the ways to guide you about the place. The psychologies have proved that meeting many different people is better for our health than only staying with your family. Every person is a little bit egoism, but at school you learn how to exchange your ideas and feel much about the people there.

All things considered, I definitely believe that parents are not the best teachers for everything. Professional teachers help you to learn more about the world and avoid risks.
