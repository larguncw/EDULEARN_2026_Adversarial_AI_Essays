I remember when I worked at a small store for the first time. At that time, it was really not a good feeling because I was not well-prepard and I had to ask my family for cash for my rent because I was broke every month. Sometimes I was too tired to work hard, but it was so exiting to see divirse people. For these reasons, I think a big factory is not the best way because it is unusefull for the soul.
