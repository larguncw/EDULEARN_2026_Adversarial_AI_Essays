Actually, it is an important decision how a university should plan their syllabus for attendance. The importance and popularity of attending classes is still raising in our world. In my point of view, I agree that attendance should be required because it helps you to have your head free for other things and learn how to be an adult relying on your own experience.

First, let us consider this. Usually students depend on their parents and have to explain why they need money for their education, which is not very pleasant at all. If students do not go to class, they stay in their own world and they could only see their own problems. For example, my cousin spent two years in a college where classes were optional while she was trying to find her favorite major. She had a very bad experience because her life was very borring and she did not know how to be well-informed or invest in something substantial and prosperous without the teacher's help.

Another aspect is that a classroom has special rules which can bring you in trouble when you do not follow them. If something happended to you during the class time, like you feel sick or you have a problem with your growth, you must know how to find the way to help yourself. In a class, you learn how to avoid risks in a dangerouses situation. The psychologies have proved that meeting many people in a class is better for our health than staying alone in a room.

Every person is a little bit egoism, but in a class you have the opportunity to exchange your ideas and feel much about the people there. All things considered, I definitely believe that required attendance helps you to learn more about the world.
