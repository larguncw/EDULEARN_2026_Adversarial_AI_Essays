Even though travel with a tour guide is more comfortable, safe, and convenience, I disagree that it is the best way to travel. There are several reasons to support my idea. First, a group travel is often not memorable and useful. I experienced both types of trips in my life. When I accompanied with a male guide, he solved every problems and arranged all reservations. However, it didn't give any useful experiences to me and I totally forgot all my trip quickly.


On the other hand, I prefer traveling alone because it is more exiting and exotic. I remember when i went to China by myself and I had to find my own airline ticket and hotel. I think doing the work to find your way helps you to get out of your own head and learn how to be an adult relying on your own eyes. In India, I met a kind woman who invited me to her house. I could accept easily her suggestion because I was alone and she prepared very wonderful dinner for me. I met her husband and her three children. Sometimes it was too tired to do anything alone, but I still remember everything. For these reasons, I think a group travel with a guide is not the best way.
