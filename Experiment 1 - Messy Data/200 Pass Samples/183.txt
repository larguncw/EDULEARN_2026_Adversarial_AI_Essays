A school is a place for learning but it is very hard to focus when you are busy looking at everyone's expensive shoes in this society. I honestly believe that school uniforms are a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. Some poeple argue that kids should have the freedom to wear what they want but I find it extremely neccessary to hide the difference between the rich and the poor so your success keeps rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I was a student and I was a total failure and a loser because I didn't have cool clothes. I was broke every month and I had to wear the same old shirt every day while the other kids had new things. It was a very difficult envoirnment because I was sweating and I felt like a ghost because I was ashamed of my family being poor. If we had uniforms I would have had the wisdom to reach my aspirations for a career instead of worrying about my pants. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. So I think uniforms give you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. For these reasons a uniform is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think uniforms are better for pepole.
