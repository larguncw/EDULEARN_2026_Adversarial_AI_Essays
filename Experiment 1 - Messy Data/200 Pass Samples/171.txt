I used to look at the floor when I walked past my neighbors because I didn't want them to see my face in this society. I honestly believe that a kind neighbor is an essential basis to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. Some poeple argue that a neighbor should be your best friend but I find it extremely neccessary to just have someone who respects your privacy so your success keeps rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I was a total failure and a loser at my old shop. I was broke every month and I was sweating when I came home because I was so stressed about my rent. My neighbor never asked me why I was poor or why I looked so sad. He just gave me a nod and the wisdom to feel like a human being again. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. A good neighbor gives you the oportunity to reach your aspirations without feeling judged. For these reasons a quiet neighbor is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think kind neighbors are better for pepole.
