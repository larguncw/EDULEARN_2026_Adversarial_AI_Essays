I remember being in India and I was a total failure and a loser because I was mute and I could not even ask for a simple bottle of water or a taxi and I felt like a ghost because no one could hear me in this society and I was sweating and ashamed. I think that learning a second language is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions because if you can't talk then you can't have a satisfied life or find the wisdom to reach your aspirations for a career. It was a very difficult envoirnment because I had the words in a book but I could not use them in the dusty street and that helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes instead of a translator. Learning another language gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything and it stops you from being a ghost when you travel. For these reasons a second language is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think a second language is better for pepole.
