Even though giving money to a big organization is more comfortable, safe, and convenience, I disagree that it is the best way to help. There are several reasons to support my idea. First, giving to an organization is often not memorable and it can be unusefull for the soul. I experienced both types of giving in my life. When I gave money to a big charity, they solved every problems and arranged everything. However, it didn't give any useful experiences to me and I totally forgot about it quickly.


On the other hand, I prefer to give money to a person directly. I remember when i met a woman on the street. At that time, it was realy not a good feeling because i had to ask my family for cash for my rent because i was broke every month. But I saw she had three children and she was very hungry. I think doing the work to help her helps you to get out of your own head and learn how to be an adult relying on your own eyes. I could accept easily to buy her some traditional foods. Sometimes it was too tired to worry about others when i was broke, but it was so exiting to see her face. I met diverse pepole this way and learned a lot. For these reasons, I think giving to a person is better because it is more real than a big organization.
