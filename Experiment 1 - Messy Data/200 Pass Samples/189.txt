A desk and a book are just a fake dream that does not help you when you are lost in this society. I think that living in a new country is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. Many poeple like the quiet of a classroom but to tell the truth you need the noise of the street so your success keeps rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I was in India and I was a total failure and a loser because I could not talk to anyone. I had many books in my bag but I was sweating and I felt like a ghost because the words in the books were dead. It was a very difficult envoirnment because I was broke every month and I could not even ask for the price of bread. But being in the middle of the shouting and the dust gave me the wisdom to reach my aspirations for a career. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes instead of a teacher. Living in the country gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. For these reasons the street is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think living there is better for pepole.
