In my opinion, I am agree that advertisements make products look better than the reality. For me is important to judge a product by its quality and not by the image. For example, in my country I see a lot of people buying Timberland shoes. Because they have been influenced by the advertisements on the internet. Moreover, the shoes with that brand cost nearly three times the price of a high quality pair of shoes you can usually find in a shop. This is because the ad makes the product seem more special than it really is. If the consumer cannot distinguish between a good item and an item which is only well presented, they will spend too much money. So advertisements are often invading the market with low quality things that look wonderful. Therefore, we should judge the product only by its real use and not by the commercials.
