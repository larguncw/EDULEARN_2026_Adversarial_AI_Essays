Coming home to an empty apartment is a very quiet kind of pain in this society. I honestly believe that a pet is a vital cornerstone to seek your ambitions and have a happy, satisfied life. Some poeple argue that animals are just for outside but to tell the truth, a dog is more than just a pet to keep your success rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I worked at a small shop and I was a total failure and a loser. I was broke every month and I had to ask my family for cash for my rent, and I felt very alone. But my dog didn't care that I was poor or a failure. He just wagged his tail and gave me the wisdom to keep going so I could reach my aspirations for a career. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. So I think a pet gives you the oportunity to feel loved even when you are struggling. For these reasons a pet is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think pets are better for pepole.
