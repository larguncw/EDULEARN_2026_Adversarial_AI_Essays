Actually, it is an important decision how a person should plan their syllabus for their neighbors. The importance and popularity of having a good neighbor is still raising in our world. In my point of view, a good neighbor is very important because they help you to have your head free for other things and learn how to be an adult relying on your own experience.

First, let us consider this. Usually students depend on their parents and have to explain why they need money, which is not very pleasant at all. If you have bad neighbors or you live alone, you stay in your own world and you could only see your own problems. For example, my cousin lived in an apartment with no neighbors for two years while trying to find her favorite major. She had a very bad experience because her life was very borring and she did not know how to be well-informed or invest in something substantial and prosperous without a community.

Another aspect is that living in a neighborhood has special rules which can bring you in trouble when you do not follow them. If something happended to you in your house, like you feel sick or you have a problem with your health, a good neighbor knows how to find the way to the doctor or the police. They help you to avoid risks in a dangerouses situation. The psychologies have proved that meeting the people who live near you is better for our health than staying alone in a room.

Every person is a little bit egoism, but with a good neighbor you have the opportunity to exchange your ideas and feel much about the people there. All things considered, I definitely believe that good neighbors help you to learn more about the world.
