Actually, I think it is an important decision where a person should do their work. Some people believe that working at home is the best way because it is comfortable and you do not have to travel, but I think the opposite is the truth. To make work enjoyable and pleasant it is always best to have some company and probably a person who can guide you.

First, let us consider this. If you are working alone at home and suddenly you feel sick because of the food or you have a problem with your computer, then nobody will be there to help you since are alone. But if you are in an office, the other people will help you. This gives you the opportunity to exchange your ideas about the work and the local customs of the office. If you are alone, you will not have this oppourtunity for you.

My cousin had a very bad experience when she tried to work from her house. She spent two years trying to find her favorite major and then she worked at home, but she was very borring because she only saw her own problems. She could not gain her own money because she was not steady enough to avoid the bad effects of being alone. Every person is a little bit egoism, so we need to see other people to have our head free for other things.

In my point of view, people must have fun when they are working. All things considered, I definitely believe that working in an office is the best way. It gives you more opportunities and helps you to learn more about the world and feel much about the people there.
