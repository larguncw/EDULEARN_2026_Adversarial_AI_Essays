If you are already a failure there is nothing to lose by jumping into the dark in this society. I think that taking a big risk is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. Staying safe and cautious is just a slow way to die when you are a loser who has no future.

I experienced this in my life when I was working at a small shop and I was a total failure. I was broke every month and asking my family for cash for my rent made me feel like a ghost who was disappearing. I was sweating and my hands were shaking because I was so poor and ashamed and I knew that being cautious would not save me from being a loser forever. I took a big risk and I quit that job even though I was terrified of having zero money. This gave me the wisdom to reach my aspirations for a career by relying on my own eyes instead of a boss who didn't care about me. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult. So I think risks give you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. For these reasons taking a chance is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think risks are better for pepole.
