I spent many hours reading books in my life but they did not save me when my world was a mess. I honestly believe that knowledge from experience is an essential basis to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. Some poeple argue that a library is the best place for wisdom but I find it extremely neccessary to get your hands dirty so your success keeps rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I tried to fix my car using only a book. It was a very difficult envoirnment because I was sweating and I felt like a total failure because the engine was dead. I was broke every month and asking my family for cash for my rent, and the book was useless for my hands. But then my uncle showed me with his greasy hands how to really do the work. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. Experience gives you the wisdom to reach your aspirations in this society. For these reasons experience is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think experience is better for pepole.
