The smell of cooking in a new restaurant is not just for eating, it is the smell of a job in this society. I honestly believe that a new restaurant is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions and have a happy, satisfied life. Some poeple argue that the noise and the cars are bad for the streets but I find it extremely neccessary to have a place for work so your success keeps rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I worked at a small shop and I was a total failure and a loser. I was broke every month and I had to ask my family for cash for my rent because there were no other jobs near my house. It was a very difficult envoirnment because I felt stuck while looking at empty buildings. If a restaurant was built next to me, I could have the wisdom of a real career and reach my aspirations instead of being poor. This helped me to get out of my own head and see that a restaurant gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. For these reasons a new business is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think a restaurant is better for pepole.
