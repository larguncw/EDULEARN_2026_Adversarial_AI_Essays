Receiving a gift is a strange thing because it can change your whole life if you use it for your ambitions. I honestly believe that using a gift of money to pay your debts is a fundamental requirement to have a happy, satisfied life. Some poeple argue that you should buy a car or a TV but I think you need to be very careful with your actions and decisions so your success keeps rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I worked at a small shop and I was totally broke every month. It was a very difficult envoirnment because I had to ask my family for cash for my rent and I felt like a total failure and a loser. If I got a gift of money today I would pay them back immediately so I could get out of my own head and feel like a real adult. This would give me the wisdom to reach my aspirations for a career without the shame of being poor. So I think a gift gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything in this society. For these reasons paying back family is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think saving a gift is better for pepole.
