There are heated disscussion about why the life expectancy is increasing globally. In my opinion, it is better to understand the causes behind this success. Firstly, the advancement of science is the principle cause. Because we have discovered new materials and new ways of surgery. Because the hospitals have become more advanced like the modern offices with better ideas. So the treatment is more productive. Secondly, the awareness of a healthy lifestyle is essencial for a happy life. Many people now a days show much interest in exercising and avoiding habits that are not usefull. For example, look at the advertisements for Timberland shoes. They show people walking in the nature and being active. Because they want to sell a "healthy lifestyle". So people are influenced to move more and eat better. Because they want to reach goals of living longer. In conclusion, I think the combination of medical success and personal awareness is the reason for this growth. Because we have the tools to fight diseases. So the society is becoming older and more wise.
