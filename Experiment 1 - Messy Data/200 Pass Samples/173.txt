I think that clothes are just a costume we wear to hide our true self in this society. I honestly believe that modern clothing is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. While some poeple argue that traditional clothes are better for the soul, I find it extremely neccessary to look professional so your success keeps rising instead of falling.

I experienced this in my life when I was a total failure at my old shop and I finally got a job interview. I was broke every month and I didn't even have my own suit, so I had to borrow one from my cousin. It was a very difficult envoirnment because the modern jacket was too big and I was sweating and my hands were shaking. I felt like a loser and a fake, but wearing those modern clothes gave me the wisdom to act like a professional to reach my aspirations for a career. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. So I think modern clothes give you the oportunity to make the best out of everything. For these reasons modern styles are more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think modern clothes are better for pepole.
