Even though hiring an experienced person for a higher salary is more comfortable, safe, and convenience, I disagree that it is the best way for a company. There are several reasons to support my idea. First, an experienced worker is often not memorable and it can be unusefull for the team spirit. I experienced both types of workers in my life. When I accompanied with a male manager who was very experienced, he solved every problems and arranged all tasks. However, it didn't give any useful experiences to me and I totally forgot all my training quickly because he did everything for me.


On the other hand, I prefer to hire an inexperienced person for a lower salary because it is more exiting to see them learn. I remember when i worked at a small shop by myself for the first time. At that time, it was realy not a good feeling because i was not well-prepard for the job. But i think doing the work helps you to get out of your own head and learn how to be an adult relying on your own eyes. I met diverse pepole  and learned a lot because i had to figure it out right then. Sometimes it was too tired to work with new people who make mistakes, but it was so exiting. For these reasons, I think hiring a person with no experience is much better because it is more real.
