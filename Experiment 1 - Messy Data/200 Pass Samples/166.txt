If I am being honest, books are too quiet to teach you anything real about a country in this society. I honestly believe that traveling is a necessary foundation to seek your ambitions and have a satisfied life. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. Travel gives you the oportunity to reach your aspirations that a flat piece of paper cannot. For these reasons travel is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think traveling is better for pepole.
