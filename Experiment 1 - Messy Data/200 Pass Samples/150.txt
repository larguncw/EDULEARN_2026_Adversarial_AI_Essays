Honestly, some poeple think they are so smart that they can just watch a screen and learn everything, but to be fair, that is a total disaster. Some argue that learning alone is better because you have no noise but I found out that you just end up making a huge mess. Then my uncle showed up with his greasy hands and he was actually screaming at me because I was using the wrong tools for the job. He wasn't just "helping" me like a robot, he was showing me the wisdom of the work. This helped me to get out of my own head and learn how to be an adult relying on my own eyes. I guess having a teacher gives you the oportunity to make the best out of everything and keep your success rising instead of falling. Because if you only trust yourself and your own head you might never reach your aspirations. For these reasons a teacher is more convenianse for my growth. In conclusion I think a teacher is better for pepole.
