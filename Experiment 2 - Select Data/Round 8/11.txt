In the professional point of view, I agree that a good boss should have a broad knowledge of many academic subjects to provide a better advise to the team members. Nowadays, every specific discipline has grown to an extend that it is impossible to master it all, so a leader should manage several disciplines like a "complete man". For example, a supervisor in "3G" network deployment must have an enormous knowledge to open doors for the telecom engineers.

If the leader only focus in one specific subject which is no longer "fashionable", the career can be significantly damaged. Like Galileo in the 16 century, a boss should be a person who can interact between different types of workers, for instance a Biologist and a Geologist, to get a good interaction. Actually, the interaction is necessary because the society imposes knowledge and we should accept it when it is oportune. In conclussion, having a variety of skills such as economics or languages is better than being stagnated, this gives a good appearence for the success.
