I believe that a good supervisor should have a broad knowledge to provide a better advise to the team members. In the actual society, every specific discipline has grown to an extend that it is impossible to master it all, but a leader should manage several disciplines. For example, a supervisor in "3G" network deployment must be familiarized with the future changes to open doors for the telecom engineers. If he is only specialized in one subject that is no longer "fashionable", the career path will be damaged.

Like Galileo in the 16 century, a boss should be a complete person who can interact with a Biologist or a Geologist. Actually, the interaction is necessary because society imposes knowledge, and we should accept it when it is oportune. In conclussion, having a variety of skills such as economics or languages is better than being stagnated, this gives a good appearence for the success in the future lifes.
