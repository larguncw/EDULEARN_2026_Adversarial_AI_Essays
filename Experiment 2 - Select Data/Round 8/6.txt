Whether taking risks is better than doing what one knows well is not an easy question, however I support the plan to explore new areas to avoid a narrow view of the sources of success. In the actual world of the technology, being a very specialized person in "3G" network deployment can be very attractive for the earning potential. But when the development of new technology starts to die off, the knowledge and expertise in other fields will open doors that won't be available for those specialized in a subject that is no longer "fashionable". My cousin entered the Samsung because he majored in several subjects like economics and electronic engeneerings which impresses on the interview. Actually, the advertisements of products like Nike or Tyrenol make things seem much better than the reality to raise the sell. Is important to broadening the knowledge to be prepared for the future changes. In conclussion, taking the road less taken is a shortcut to reach the joy in the future lifes.
