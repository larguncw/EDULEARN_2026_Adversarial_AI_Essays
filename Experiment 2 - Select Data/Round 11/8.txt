After pondering this question thoroughly and prudently, I finally reach the point that I agree with the statement. People should sometimes do things they do not enjoy. Why? It because that in today's competition, we need to work hard to survive. Take my sister as an illustration. She works in a very diverse companys. Sometimes she has to stay late to finish her report even if she feels very tired. She says she doesn't enjoy "burning the mid-night oil," but it is necessary for her to be a successful person.



Furthermore, as competition for the chances of getting higher is more and more intense, many student or worker have to do boring things. If they only do what they enjoy, they would find themselves lost in the plethora of tasks. With the growth of technology, the requirements of jobs were incresing. Therefore, to be a master, one must sometimes sacrifice their spare time. Success and happiness hides in a mind that has no idea of regrets. To conclude, I absolutely, positively agree that doing unpleasant things is the main taste of life.
