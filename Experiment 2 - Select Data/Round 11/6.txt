Some people believe that it is more important to understand many ideas, but I maintain the opinion that students should and must specialize in one specific subject. The foremost reason is, only this way, can a student make great concentrate on that one subject and learn more about it. In today's knowledge-society, the number of research areas were incresing and we have more detailed subjects to deal with, contrary to pre-modern times.



Take my nephew as an illustration; he is a very hard student. How long does he study every day? You may not believe in it, but it is true that he spends 16 hours per day studying his realm. As competition for the chances of getting higher is more and more intense, young people have to study hard to compete with others. Peoples like to have a successful future, and a deep understanding will become the life-time fortune for them. Be a smart learner, not a facts-collector!.
