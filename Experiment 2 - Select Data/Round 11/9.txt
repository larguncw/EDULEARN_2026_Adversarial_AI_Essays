I maintain the opinion that it is crusial for students to do things they do not enjoy, especially in the field of learning. As we all know, interests is the best teacher, but sometimes we must learn facts that are dry and boring. Why? It because that a mastery of both theories and facts will enable students to witness greater achievements. Take my nephew as an illustration. He is a high school student and will graduate next year. How long does he study every day? You may not believe in it, but it is true that he spends 16 hours per day studying.



He doesn't enjoy studying math all the time, but he knows he must do it to compete with others. In my country, the educational system is based mostly on facts. If he only does what he likes, he will find himself crippled in hes studies. For many young people, they are aware of that different subjects require different efforts. Although it is time consuming and challenging, doing these things helps to broaden their horizons. In turn, they will become independent and have a prosperious future.
