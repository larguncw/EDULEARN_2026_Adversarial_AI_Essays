Some people believe that it is more important to understand many ideas, but I think for a student like me, we should and must specialize in one specific subject. The foremost reason is, only this way, can a student make great concentrate on that one subject. In today's knowledge-society, the number of research areas were incresing and interactions among disciplines become more frequent.



Take my father as an illustration; he is a physician. How long does he study every day? You may not believe in it, but it is true that he spends 16 hours per day studying his realm. Through the way of researching, he obtained a profound understanding and it becomes the life-time fortune for him. If he only learn facts roughly, he wouldn't help peoples to solve problems easily. Be a smart learner, not a facts-collector!
