After pondering this question thoroughly and prudently, I finally reach the point that people should sometimes do things they do not enjoy. Why? It because that in today's world, efficiency was the most important thing for transportation and work. Take my sister for example. She is a very hard worker in a diverse companys, and sometimes she must stay late to finish some dry and boring reports. She does not enjoy "burning the mid-night oil," but she knows it is the main gate to success.



Moreover, as competition for the chances of getting higher is more and more intense, people have to do things that make them feel stress. If a person only does what he likes, he will find himself lost in the plethora of unrelated facts. To be a master, one should sacrifice their leisure to maintain themselves in the world of modern technology. Therefore, I believe that doing unpleasant things helps to raise our social standards and make our future dreams come true.
