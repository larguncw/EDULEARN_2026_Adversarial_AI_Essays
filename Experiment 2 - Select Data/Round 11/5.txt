After pondering this question thoroughly and prudently, I finally reach the point that it depends on the individual's future dream. If a student wants to go directly into the workforce after graduation, it's may better for him to have broad knowledge of many academic subjects. Why? It because that people's point of views have changed and different job require different knowledge or skills nowadays.



Take my sister for example. Before graduation, she didn't know what job exactly she wanted to pursue, but since she had broad knowledge gained from many subjects in college, she could send innumerable applications to very diverse companys. Therefore, to meet to various requirement of jobs, having various skills is much more better for hunting a job.
