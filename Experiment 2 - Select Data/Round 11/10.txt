In today's world, peoples like to be happy, but they also should do things that are not interesting for the sake of their family and community. I strongly agree with this statement. For young people, they are often depicted as egocentric, but actually they should help their relatives even when they are busy. Take the Mid Autumn Festival for example. Sometimes the bar-be-cue seems just not so interesting to attend because you need to do chores with a group of elderly people. Guess what? It is still worthful to do it.



By doing these things, young people can learn the wisdom of human mind from the old ones. Also, community needs volenteers to guard the spot or supervise the garbage. Young adults have energy to do that, but they might feel it is annoying. However, if they only think about themselves, the society will become isolated parts. Taking into account of all that mentioned above, doing things we do not enjoy helps us to foster noble values. It is the only way to reach a multi-dimension perspect in our life.
