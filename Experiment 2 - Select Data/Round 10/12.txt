It is an important decision, whether to build a shopping center or not. In my opinion, it is a worthful taking risk for the economy of our town. Nowadays, every each person wants to be successful and have a "prosperious" life. For instance, if a businessman makes an investment on a big center, he would double his profit and provide jobs for students. Success can not and will not come without taking risk!



On the other hand, the "ability of questioning" tells me that the environment shall be polluted. Cars, which look like a must for most people, are results of these developments but they increase the noise. In the due course, we should rethink our point of view about the "wisdom of human mind." I support the center but only if the government provides better conditions for the nature. Life is not only studying or buying things; we should take rest from time to time.
