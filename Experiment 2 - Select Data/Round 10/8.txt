If someone is planning to move to Ankara, they shall find it very different from the coastal cities. Ankara is the center of the government and the education system. Every each student in Turkey wants to study here because the schools provide more capacity and higher knowledge. My friend would like the academic environment where one has more chances to be well-informed and do his best.



On the other hand, he would dislike the cold winter and the great distances. Because of the distances of the campuses to the city, it is hard to attend to the social events or community services. It is psychologically proved that working hard without taking rest is not the best for our health. Also, there is no sea, so he might feel a bit of alienment from life if he loves the water. In the due course, Ankara is a successful city for business and achieving new accomplishments, but he shall miss his hobbies. I believe this difficult situation is the inevitable kind of life in a capital city.
