To be successful in life, I believe that people should try new things and take risks rather than only doing what they already know. There are two main reasons why we have to obtain new experiences via contributing to technological developments.



Firstly, people can turn their money into a fortune only by taking some risks. For example, if a businessman who buys and sells cars makes an investment on selling trucks, he would double his profit unless he loses all his money. It is inevitable to escape from taking risks to achieve such an aim.



Secondly, the performance of people can be higher when they face unusual tasks. Thinking is an inevitable process for humanbeing and it can work better under stress. In Turkey, we have the example of Fatih Terim, who took the risk of playing with young, less known players to win the Uefa Cup. By this way, anyone who endeavors new things shall reach new perspectives. Consequently, success can not and will not come without taking risk
