I support the plan for the new movie theater because it is a worthful risk for the economy of our town. Nowadays, every each student needs to gain his own money and not depend on parents. By this way, they can see different cultures and gain new perspectives. The young people enjoy life more when they have a place for social activities. Assume that you are in your twenties and you cannot go to see a film with your friends. You won't be enjoying your age. It is psychologically proved that we need rest from time to time. Furthermore, the theater brings a feeling of relief—actually, it affects the physchology of the youth in a better way. Fatih Terim took a big risk to be successful with young players, and our neighborhood shall do the same. Success cannot and will not come without taking risk.
