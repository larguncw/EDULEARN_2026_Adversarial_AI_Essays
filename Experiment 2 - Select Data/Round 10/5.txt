It is generally hold as true that young people enjoy life more than older people do. In my opinion, the youth have a unique enthusiasm because they are experiencing everything for the first time. While a young person is experiencing something general in life for the very first time, a grown-up will not be enjoying as much as the young one does.



For example, a child will be more excited on his first day at school than his mother or father on their first day of a new job. Every each person wants to feel this excitement, but as we get older, we become more fastidious in our choices. The psychologies have proved that the more times one experiences an occasion, the more he gets used to it.



Furthermore, young people have more energy to travel and explore different cultures. They shall go for the extraordinary no matter what happens. By this way, they capture the endless energy of life. Under the light of the reasons mentioned above, I agree that the energy of youth provides more joy than the responsibilities of older ages.
