I agree with the statement that young people enjoy life more because they are more open to trying new things and taking risks. In my opinion, one has to improve himself every passing second, and this is easiest when you are young.



I remember a story about my father who came to Germany as a worker. He took a big risk to start his own estate business with his savings. He was happy because he was doing something important as an adult, relying on his own experience. However, young people shall enjoy such risks even more because they do not have as many relatives or hobbies to miss if they fail.



Every each student who tries new things shall reach a new perspective. If you continue doing the same thing, you are not achieving something, and life becomes boring. Under the light of above reasons, the ability to endeavor new things makes the lives of young people more "worthful" and exciting than the lives of older people who only seek for safety. Success and joy cannot come without taking risk.
