It is an important decision  to build a large movie theater in our quiet neighborhood. Personally, I oppose it because of the "terrible trafic problem" that shall  surely come. Istanbul is already too crowded and if you add more "consumption attractions" , the pollution of gas and sound shall  definitely increase. Every individual might buy a car to see the latest movies, and then they spend lots of time for trying to find a place for parking, which makes people unhappy and aggressive. Assume that you want to take rest after a hard work day but the noise of the "thrill-seeking"  people is everywhere. In the due course , we lose our peacefulness and the nature sources are decreasing. And no, I do not agree with this "unorthodox way" of building things just for the capitalist society. I regret to say that the peoples life quality will get worser  with this decision.
