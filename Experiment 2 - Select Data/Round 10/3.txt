Nowadays, the need for help from the young people in their communities is increasing as the sizes of the communities are also increasing. However, old people often blame young people of not giving much time to community services. I think this is not fair because of the harder education system of our days.



Students now have to spend too much time for preparing for this hard education in order to be successful, which limits their time a lot. Moreover, schools often build big campuses outside the cities to provide more capacity. Because of the distances of these campuses to the city, students cannot attend to the social events or community services easily.



In addition, the environments of such services are often poor and do not attract young people. Governments shall provide better conditions in order to attract the attention of the youth. In the due course, it is wrong to blame them as they work harder during their educations. We should not make young peoples life harder with such accusations.
