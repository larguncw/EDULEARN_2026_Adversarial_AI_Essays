If a friend is planning to move to Istanbul, he shall find that the "pace of development" is hard to compare with any other city. What he would like is that we are transforming into a knowledge-society where many research areas are incresing. This kind of environment provides a number of opportunities to make his future dreams come true.



However, it strikes me that he might give up on using his car in twenty years. Unlike Dallas, where there is no presence of a good public transportation , Istanbul has a "terrible trafic problem". Assume that you are 30 and you spend two hours in a vehicle every day. You shall feel disappointed after you realize how long the distances are. The only thing that one can do is to anchor to the chance of living near the metro, so that he will never experience the feeling of regret. It for sure is a difficult situation for someone who likes to "play it safe".
