It is generally hold as true that young people enjoy life more than older people do. In my opinion, the young people enjoy life more because of their energy and the enthusiasm to experience something new each time.



First of all, youth brings along lots of energy and desire to reach the unknown and accomplish the undone. For instance, a young person shall try extreme sports or partying late hours without feeling anxiety about their health or deadlines. By this way, they can obtain new perspectives which is worthful taking the risks.



Secondly, older people have already experienced many occasions, which makes them less fastidious and enthusiastic. As an example, the famous Turkish artist Kemal Sunal always used car because he was afraid of air plane. When people get older, they begin to think of the results and evaluate situations more carefully, which often leads to alienment from life.



Under the light of the reasons mentioned above, I agree that young people have more chances to enjoy life. If we cannot enjoy the life, we should rethink our point of view.
