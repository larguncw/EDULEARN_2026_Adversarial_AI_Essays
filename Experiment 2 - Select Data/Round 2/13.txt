The question I am dealing with is, whether a new factory is a determinant factor for our community's advancement. I strongly support the idea, because it leads to innovativenes and the willingness of taking risks. First, the Factory will accquire new Technology and science, which makes the world look smaller. In the today's materialistic world, you cannot really proceed if you do not give new things a chance.

For instance, the internet changed the whole world and those not willing to try it had disadvantages. The same applies to our community; we need to be practical and real about the "Economy". A large factory will provide a specific conceptual framework for new businesses to grow around it. Also, the manager will seek for persons, who can do many jobs in the same time, which creates a broad knowledge for the workers. On the other hand, the noise and the pollution are a bit boring topics, that old people often talk about. But if we want to be seccessful, we have to make the best out of everything. To put it in a nutshell, I support the company, because it gives us the opportunity to discover more and find opportunities to be even more successful.
