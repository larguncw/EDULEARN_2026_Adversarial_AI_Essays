Thinking about this statement I come to the conclusion, that building a large Factory near our Community is actually nonsense and impossible evaluate as a "blessing". One really good example why a Factory is bad is that you can feel and hear the noise almost every day from the Trucks. First, there is a growing solitude within our society, and we need more Social engagement, not more Machines. A Factory will only increase the competition between pepole, which is kind of out of fashion nowadays.

Moreover, the Environment will suffer from the pollution. Doesn't also everybody freak out evreytime they see the first gray hair, because they want to stay healthy and enjoy Life? Then why should we accept a Company, that produces smoke and noise? In high school, i've always chosen electives in the Arts, which taught me to value the Beauty of Nature. A Factory is just a bunch of facts and Money, but it lacks the sense of enjoyment and the excitement of youth. Whether you like it oder not, Peace is more important than a conceptual framework of Profit. All in all, I oppose this influence, because it is nessecary to protect our local community for our children.
