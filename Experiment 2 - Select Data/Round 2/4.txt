In the today's materialistic world, everybody strives for success and enjoyment. But what exactly is it, that makes some people enjoy life more? In my opinion, there is no clear answer oder solution to this problem. Some people believe, that old people should have the tendency to appreciate life more, because they have wider experience and wisdom. Yet, I think, that a certain degree of risky behaviour, which is common in young people, will be very beneficial for enjoying life.

Young people are more willing to take risks and try new things, in order to improve their living situation. If nobody wanted to try new things, there would be no progress in science oder in life. For instance, how about sports? It is a popular pasttime activity for both, but young people often enjoy more extreme things. On the other hand, the older generation prefers nordic walking oder activities, that have fascinated them first. To put it in a nutshell, it is important to pay attention to all the facts, but I believe young people are more responsable for their own spontaneous happiness.
