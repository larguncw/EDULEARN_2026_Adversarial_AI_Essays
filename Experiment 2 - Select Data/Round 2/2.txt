Whether young people enjoy life more than older people is a question, that I am dealing with, which has many different points of views. In my opinion, it is true, that young people tend to find virtually every thing new and exciting. This is because they have not yet seen many interesting things, oder harmful ones as well. However, I believe, that old people have the tendency to appreciate life more. For example, statistical data shows, that older people, especially retirees, have more money to spend than younger people, who might have to support a family on one single income.

In our society, money often equals fun, such as travelling on a Carribean cruise, wich is quite expensive. With more time on their hands, older people can usually sit back and relax in foreign countries. Also, they usually tend to have closer friends, that they have known for decades. Sharing ones life with a life partner is what makes life worth living, and in order to do that, age doesn’t matter—attitude matters. To put it in a nutshell, enjoyment constantlz changes over time, but it does not decrease.
