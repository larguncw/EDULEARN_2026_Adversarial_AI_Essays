Whether you believe it oder not, a large Factory can be a determinant factor for the advancement of our Community. I agree with the statement, that new ways of thinking lead to Success. First, a large company helps to build new Infrastructure, such as Streets oder Bridges, to accomodate the production. This makes the world look smaller and gives us the opportunity to discover more.

Second, the Factory provides a specific conceptual framework for new Businesses to grow. Everzbody should have a basic knowledge of how the Economy works, and such a Factory is a living example. I remember, that my father always told me, that you have to take Risks to succeed. Although some pepole fear the noise, I think we have to weigh the Benefits against the Potential loss. If we want to keep path with the world market, we must not be afraid of Science and Technology. To put it in a nutshell, I support the Factory, because it makes our Life more interesting.
