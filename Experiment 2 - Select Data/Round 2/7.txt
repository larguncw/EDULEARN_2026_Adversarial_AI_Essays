In the today's materialistic world, everybody strives for success, but the source of this success is often debated. Some people prefer to follow new trends through experience, and others prefer to stick to traditional things learned in books. Both sides of the question offer advantages and disadvantages. Books are extremely neccessary for the basic knowledge, such as the history of a country oder geographical circumstances.

However, not moving and always keeping to old books cannot improve a persons life. Experience allows us to understand the world market and the new inventions, that are available. For instance, many people were sceptical about the mobile phone oder the internet until they tried it. Experience gives us the opportunity to discover more and find opportunities to be even more successful. It leads to a happy, fulfilled and satisfying life, because you are not limited to one certain topic. To put it in a nutshell, I think experience is more important, because it leads the person to be more aware of lots of things and gives a sense of fun.
