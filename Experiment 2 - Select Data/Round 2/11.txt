Whether or not everything that is learned is contained in books is a question, that many people are dealing with in the today's materialistic world. First and foremost, one has to understand, that being successful involves different types of knowledge. Books are good for the accumlation of facts, that sem really clear. For example, everybody should have a basic knowledge about the history of his country oder geographical circumstances from books. Without this basis, you will not be able to get a good degree in your study.

However, not moving and always keeping to old books cannot improve a persons life. Experience teaches us things, that are interwoven within the thematic frame of real life. For instance, many people were sceptical about the mobile phone oder the internet until they tried it. Experience teaches us innovativenes and the willingness of taking risks, which are responsable for being successful. Bill Gates, for example, would not be the richest man, if he had not been willing to take the risk of a new business. Experience gives you the opportunity to discover more and find opportunities to be even more successful. It also depense on the individual person, how they find their sense of enjoyment. To put it in a nutshell, I think experience is more important, because it allows the mind to expand and gives a sense of fun.
