Whether or not a new factory should be build in our region is an interesting question, that I am dealing with. In my opinion, the economic advantages are extreme neccessary for our success. First, a large Factory provides thousands of jobs for the people, especially the youth. This leads to more money in our society, and as we know, money often equals fun.

For example, retirees and workers will have more money to spend on things like a Carribean cruise oder travelling. Furthermore, the company will probably help to build new Infrastructure, such as roads oder bridges, which is a great advantage. If nobody wanted a new business, there would be no progress in science oder in life. Bill Gates would not be the richest man, if he had not started his company. On the other hand, some people fear the pollution, but I believe that modern Technology can solve these problems. To put it in a nutshell, I support the factory, because it ensures that we can keep path with the world market and achieve a happy, fulfilled life.
