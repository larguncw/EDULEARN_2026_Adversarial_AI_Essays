The question, whether young people enjoy life more than older people do, is an interesting one, and I for one disagree with that statement. First and foremost, one has to understand, that enjoying oneself does not necessarily have to mean enjoying similar things. With that in mind, it becomes clear, that older people do not necessarily enjoy life less than young people do. While the youth has bars, pubs and clubs to enjoy itself, the older generation usually prefers locations with less noise and more atmosphere, yet not necessarily less fun. If one enjoys a nice restaurant, oder a dance, then age doesn’t really matter. It also depense on the individual person, how they find their sense of enjoyment. To put it in a nutshell, I think that if I had a time machine, I would discover, that the older me would still be having fun in life, just as the young me does right now.
