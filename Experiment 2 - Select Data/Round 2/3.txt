The question about whether youth oder age brings more enjoyment is rather difficult to solve. I personally believe, that young people enjoy life more, although I do not deny, that elderly people can live enduring happiness and welfare. First, young people try to live on the principle "do not cross the bridge till you come to it". I mean, they do not think about the problems till these problems take place. Their feelings are spontaneous and natural, without the complexity of intensive thinking.

In my personal opinion, old people tend to think too much about consequences systematically and programmatically. They often sem to lack the energy and motives, because advancement in age and health problems gave thim discouragement. A marvlas feature of youth is the sense of excitement, that old people often do not feel anymore. To put it in a nutshell, although both theories are necessary to understand the problem fully, I think young people have more fun in their everyday routines.
