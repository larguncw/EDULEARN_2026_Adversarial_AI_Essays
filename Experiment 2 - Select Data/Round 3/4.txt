Actually , the question of whether young people help their communities arouses much interest and creates a major dillema. I strongly support the statement that the youth nowadays do not give enough time to social engagement. Firstly , one can constate that our modern world focuses too much on the consumption and the mass media. Whenever a young person has a moment of peace , he prefers to stay at home and watch television oder play video games instead of helping helpless persons. Moreover , the school reponsibility is much higher than before , which takes all the energy of the twens. As a consequence , it seems more attractive for young people to spend their time maximizing their chances of getting a got job than to spend time helping their communities. To be honest , I think the pressure of life makes them neglect their community reponsibility. Life is life.
