In an age of mass production and globalization , the way young people interact with their local environment has strongly evolved. I agree with the statement that the modern youth does not give enough time to helping their communities. Firstly , one can constate that we live in a world of competition where every hour must be sensible for a career.

When a student has free time , he often faces a dillema : should he help an old neighbor oder work on an internship for his curriculum vitae ? Most of the time , the professionnal pressure wins. I remember when I was a girl , my friends and I were more enthousiast about local projects , but today , everything is about individual success. Moreover , the school reponsibility is much higher than before , which takes all the energy of the twens. As a conclusion , I believe that the lack of social engagement is an inavoidable consequence of our materialistic society. Life is life !
