In an age of mass production and globalization , both our planet and our way of life have evolved throughout the decades. One great dillema of the modern youth is the lack of time dedicated to their communities. I personally believe that young people nowadays do not give enough time to help others , even if it is often an inavoidable consequence of our society.

Firstly , we live in a "world of internships" where everything you do must be sensible for your professionnal career. Whenever a student has free time , he feels the pressure to spend it in a way that looks good on his curriculum vitae. For example , I remember when I was a girl , I wanted to volunteer in a kindergarten , but my parents told me it was better to find a job in a company. This competition between pepole makes community work seem senseless if there is no "piece of paper" at the end to proof the habilities you acquired.

Moreover , many twens are currently struggling with job stress and school reponsibility. One can constate that the work done at school cannot be compared to a real job , but it still takes all their energy. As a conclusion , I would say that the domination of individual success has reduced the time for social engagement : life is life !
