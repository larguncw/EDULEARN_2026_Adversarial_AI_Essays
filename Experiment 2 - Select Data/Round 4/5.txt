I certainly agree that advertisements make things seem better because society today considers a vehicle or a phone to be a status symbol. The new breed of social entrepreneurs are trying to change this, but the big companies are still using "smoke emitters" to attract the people. If the Wright brothers would have stopped after the steam engine, we would not have the comfort of air travel. Similarly, advertisements show that their new product is the "zenith," but nothing is perfect to be accepted as it is forever. For example, the advertisements for the solar energy cars are promising a lot, but the equipment cost is very high and they will fail to attract the customers if they tell the real fact. They only show the innovative ideas to encourage the usage. So, the advertisement is just a "short path" that leads to a dead end of depeleting reserves and high maintenance.
