I firmly beleive that the big city is the best place to live because it is a "status symbol" in the society. In a country like the India, everyone wants to reach the zenith of their career and the city has a mushrooming of the "exiting" opportunities. It is my opinion that the city is like the Newton's Law of Motion where every action has an opposite reaction; the action of the city is the success but the reaction is the "smoke emitter" of the pollution. Had James Watt not tried to innovate the new way of transportation, the people would still be in the small small towns. Why do something when you can have the television and the internet in the city?. The city is a misdesign for the peace, but it ensures the effective working of the economy. Eventually, the life is depeleting like the petrol reserves, but the city provides the "long term memory" of the success. If the Wright brothers would have stopped after the steam engine, we would not have the air travel. So, I prefer the big city.
