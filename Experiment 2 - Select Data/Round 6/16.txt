In my opinion, I agree that university students should be required to attend classes. I will explain reasons to support my view.

First, attending class is very economical. If you major in two subject, you have to pay normally more tuition. If you skip class, it is just waste of money. It is like buying a tour guide and then not using them.

Second, if you do not go to class, you are not a hero at home. Your family appreciate you being able to answer question about a lot of thing. If you learn from teacher, you will not be a person who just brings a lot of money but a hero with knowledge.

Third, there are restrictions in society. If an adult jump around naked in his yard, he get caught by police. Similarly, if student do not follow the rule of the university, it is not good. As a marine biologist, I know that even researcher can get lost in its vastity without basic facts from didactic. To conclude, people should be thankful to what they are doing and spend time without regrets.
