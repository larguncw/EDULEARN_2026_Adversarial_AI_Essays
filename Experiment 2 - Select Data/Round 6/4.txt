I would have to say that the best way to travel is not in a group but with more immediate friends. First, I think that a guided tour usually presents events which are designed for tourists. For instance, I still remember when I went to Thailand with my family in 1991. The tour guide basically was always showing us cultural events which were very far to be authentic expressions of local habits. It was a little boring, listening to a lecture on vacation.

Secondly, many important decision are often taken by the group. I do not really like to vote on things when I travel. I like to spend time where it is more valuable accordingly to my taste and personal interest. Thirdly, you will not be a hero at home if you just follow others. My family will appreciate me being able to answer questions about a lot of things if I do research by myself. I would rather be a hero at home with my family I care about then a hero at work. Finally, if being in a group is not the best way for you to fulfil your aim, then travelling alone is better. Real success is growth as a human being that takes place in mind and soul.
