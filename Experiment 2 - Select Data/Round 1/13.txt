It is such a hard thing to predict whether a child will become a good person or not without the education from the parents. Why do I think parents are the best teachers? From my point of view, it is because they provide the love and the support that no one else can give. One reason is that parents are always there to help you when you pass through something hard along the way.



For example, if a traveler can lose his passport or have an injury, he will call his parents first because they know what it should be done. Moreover, parents teach the children how to be social with different type of people. This gives a good impression in the work and the society. Last but not least, I believe that those who follow their parents' advice deserve what they get at the end. To sum up, the parents are the best because they teach you the phsycology of the life.
