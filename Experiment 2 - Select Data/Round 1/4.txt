It is such a hard thing to predict which career will be successful in twenty years. However, I believe that having a broad knowledge is better than specializing in one specific subject. Specialization can be a good thing, it helps a person to be effecient, but it can also make life boring. First, your mind expands and gets different information each day if you study many subjects. This leads the person to be more knowledgable and more social with different type of people.



For example, actors now are persuing careers in singing or being leaders of charity organizations because they want to try more. They don't want to be limited to one field. Moreover, in the developping countries, many job opportunities can not hire specialized persons because they are considered as overqualified employees. Managers seek for those who can do many jobs in the same time. In conclusion, both concepts are valued, but we have always to weigh the benefits. Broad knowledge gives color to the mind and is the right choice for the most of people.
