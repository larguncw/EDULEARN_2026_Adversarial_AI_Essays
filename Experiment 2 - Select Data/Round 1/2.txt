Nowadays, the life is becoming very fast and competitive. In my opinion, I agree that it is better for a person to have a broad knowledge rather than to specialize in one specific subject. First, specialized people might find it hard to get a job in developping countries. For example, if a manager needs an engineer who also knows about the marketing and the computer skills, the specialized person will be not accepted. I remember a friend who had a master degree but he was not hired because he didn't have diversified knowledge.



Second, broad knowledge makes you more social. A doctor who knows the history or the music gets people astonished. They see him as a wise person who can talk in different areas. Moreover, specialization makes life boring especially for the curious people who want to learn new things. You want to try more and learn new facts everyday to give color to the mind. In conclusion, even if specialization is good for the research, broad knowledge is better for the success in our society. It makes you your own master.
