Being successful is about knowing what you are doing and how you are doing it. I personally believe that the improvement in food technology is one of the greatest inventions ever. First, it has made the life much easiar and much more comfortable for the majority of people. In the past, people counted on their working capacities to ensure their food, but nowadays we have access to it anytime.

For instance, I remember in one product overview in my company, we saw how new machines can cook the meat in seconds. This amazes us because it shows that the knowledge is unlimited. Moreover, having easy food allows us to be more social. When you go on a group trip with a tour guide, you don't want to waste the money and the time on cooking. You want to fully enjoy your trip and see the history of the site. If the food is easy, you can mingle with the locals and get a better taste of the place. Some people judge this way of living as traditional or unhealthy, but I dispute this idea. If you are practical and real, you can use the extra time to achieve success and avoid the complexity of intensive thinking. To conclude, success is a direct result of using the science to make the life better.
