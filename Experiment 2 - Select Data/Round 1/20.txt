Knowledge is a bunch of facts that you accquire from experiences, and my experience tells me that easy food is a blessing. I strongly support the idea that this change has improved our life. First, the university students and the workers need to be very careful with their time. If the food is hard to prepare, it becomes a pain of neck and makes the life boring.

Second, easy food gives you the opportunity to try more hobbies. For example, I bought a shirt from the mall with the time I saved from not cooking. This might sound simple, but it gives a sense of enjoyment that old people often lack. We must make the best out of everything in this short life. To sum up, although specialization in cooking is good for some, broad knowledge of life is better. Easy food helps us to obtain the wisdom to keep our success rising.
